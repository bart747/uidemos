Password strength indicator

Since we want to keep it responsive (screen agnostic), a long bar aside won't cut it. We need something more compact.

Another issue is complicating users life. We shouldn't create password requirements that produce a lot of friction. No one is filling in forms for fun. It's an unfortunately unavoidable step.

There has to be a sane balance between ease of creating, memorizing and difficulty of cracking. For instance, passwords in a form of inside jokes are often better than wierd code.

My assumption here is that once a password meets basic requirements (8+ signs including a number), the bar is set to medium. If it's much longer and got a special character or an uppercase letter the bar says strong.
