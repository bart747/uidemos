Mastheads

Mastheads are pieces of content on the top of a home page. It's where the headline and sub-headline/bullet-points with your selling proposition are presented.

The main goal is to make it impactful — catch the visitor's eye and clearly present the message

Other issue is to create it without any extra fonts or fancy scripts. With half-decent wi-fi and cheap smartphones they will slow down the performance.
